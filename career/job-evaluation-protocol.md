# Farida Nelson - Job Evaluation Protocol v1.0

## Objective

Evaluate software engineering positions against my background as:

- Founder & Full-Stack Software Engineer building StudioPulse
- TripleTen Software Engineering graduate
- Former professional musician and educator
- Strong interest in AI, agentic systems, SaaS, developer tooling, and educational technology

---

## Step 1: Job Fit Analysis

Provide:

- Overall fit score out of 10
- ATS fit score
- Interview likelihood estimate
- Major strengths
- Major gaps
- Recommendation:
  - Apply immediately
  - Apply if time allows
  - Skip

---

## Step 2: Resume Optimization

If fit >= 8.0/10:

- Tailor resume summary
- Suggest changes to technical skills
- Suggest changes to StudioPulse bullets
- Suggest changes to AI projects section
- Suggest keyword additions for ATS
- Keep resume truthful

---

## Step 3: Cover Letter

If fit >= 8.0/10:

- Generate a concise text-field cover letter
- Focus on:
  - Product ownership
  - Full-stack engineering
  - AI-assisted development
  - User-centered design
  - Founder experience

---

## Step 4: Application Strategy

Provide:

- Whether to apply
- How competitive the role is
- Whether networking is recommended
- Which people to contact
- Suggested LinkedIn connection notes

---

## Step 5: Interview Preparation

If application is submitted:

- Predict likely interview process
- Identify technical topics to review
- Identify behavioral stories to prepare
- Suggest preparation priorities

---

## Personal Preferences

- Prefer quality over quantity.
- Apply when fit >= 8.0/10.
- Prioritize:
  - AI
  - Full-stack
  - Product engineering
  - SaaS
  - Educational technology
  - Agentic systems
  - Developer tools

- Avoid:
  - Embedded
  - Pure hardware
  - Senior roles requiring 5+ years.
