# Behavioral Interview Preparation

This section contains reusable behavioral interview answers and STAR stories.

## Planned Topics

- Tell me about yourself
- Why software engineering?
- Why are you transitioning from music?
- Why StudioPulse?
- Greatest achievement
- Biggest challenge
- Failure and lessons learned
- Leadership examples
- Team conflict resolution
- Learning new skills quickly
- Why this company?

## Behavioral Preparation Principles

- Use the STAR framework:
  - Situation
  - Task
  - Action
  - Result

- Maintain reusable stories that can be adapted to multiple interviews.

- Focus on:
  - ownership
  - learning
  - resilience
  - leadership
  - communication
  - problem solving
