# About Myself

I am a full-stack software engineer and the founder of StudioPulse, a SaaS platform designed to support music teachers, students, and parents. Before transitioning into software engineering, I spent more than 20 years as a professional musician, performer, and educator, building a career centered around teaching, creativity, and continuous learning.

Over the past two and a half years, I have focused on building production software applications using React, Node.js, TypeScript, MongoDB, and cloud technologies. Through developing StudioPulse, I have gained hands-on experience designing and implementing full-stack systems, building secure authentication and role-based access control, designing scalable data models, deploying applications to cloud infrastructure, and working directly with users to iterate on product features.

More recently, I have developed a strong interest in artificial intelligence systems, agentic workflows, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), semantic search, and AI-assisted productivity tools. I am particularly interested in exploring how AI can enhance education, improve human productivity, and create more personalized and effective learning experiences.

My background in music and education has shaped my approach to engineering: I value discipline, creativity, empathy for users, and long-term thinking. I enjoy building products that solve real problems and continuously learning new technologies and paradigms as the software industry evolves.
