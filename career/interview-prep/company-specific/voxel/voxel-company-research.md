# Voxel Company Research

## Company Overview

Voxel is an AI-powered workplace safety platform that uses existing security camera infrastructure to identify unsafe behaviors and operational risks in industrial environments.

Rather than installing specialized hardware, Voxel analyzes video streams from existing cameras using computer vision and machine learning models.

Industries include:

- Warehousing
- Manufacturing
- Logistics
- Supply chain operations

---

## Core Technology Areas

### Computer Vision

Voxel's platform appears to rely on:

- Object detection
- Human pose estimation
- Activity recognition
- Scene understanding
- Event detection

Examples:

- Person entering restricted area
- Unsafe forklift operation
- Missing safety equipment
- Dangerous worker posture
- Near-collision detection
